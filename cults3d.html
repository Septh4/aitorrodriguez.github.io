About the Collection

My Cults3D profile hosts a growing collection of 3D printable models — from tabletop miniatures and wargaming props to custom designs for clients. What started as creating assets for my own games evolved into a community resource with 500+ followers and thousands of downloads.

Each model is designed with printability in mind: optimized supports, tested tolerances, and clear assembly instructions where needed. I focus on models that fill gaps in existing product lines — the weird stuff, the niche props, the things you can't buy off the shelf.
