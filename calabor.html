About the Project

Calabor was a live-action roleplay event series that I co-organized for 5 years in the Garrotxa region. What started as a small gathering of friends evolved into a recurring community event with dozens of participants, elaborate storylines, and handcrafted props and costumes.

As one of the main organizers, I was responsible for game system design, scenario writing, event logistics, and prop fabrication. Each event featured original narratives set in a custom fantasy world, with player-driven stories and tactical combat encounters.

The experience taught me everything about designing interactive experiences: balancing player agency with narrative structure, creating satisfying combat mechanics, managing large groups, and building immersive physical environments with limited resources.
