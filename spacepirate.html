The Commission

This was a project commissioned by a friend who was completing his game design degree with a specialization in Unreal Engine 5. For his final project, he needed to build a complete game — but instead of downloading generic asset packs, he asked me to handle the worldbuilding, spaceship design, and weapon concepts.

The game directive was simple: a space invaders-style shooter where you play as a rebel fighting against the classic interstellar megacorporation. Clean premise, lots of room for visual identity.

Concept Art 3D Modeling Worldbuilding Vehicle Design Weapon Design Blender

Concept Design

I started by sketching — exploring shapes for ships, drones, and weapon modules. At the time I was reading William Gibson's Neuromancer, so I named the protagonist's ship "Winterflower" as an homage to Wintermute, one of the Tessier-Ashpool AIs.

"The sky above the port was the color of television, tuned to a dead channel."
— William Gibson, Neuromancer

Initial concept sketches — exploring ship silhouettes, weapon modules, and drone variants. Design notes include weapon types (laser, projectile, missile) and functional considerations.

3D Assets

After my collaborator selected his favorite concepts, I moved into Blender to model the final assets. Everything was delivered as FBX and OBJ files, game-ready for Unreal Engine 5.
